# Getting started

**Pokémon TCG Pocket** (PTCGP) is a mobile card game built around collecting
Pokémon Trading Card Game cards and playing quick, streamlined matches. This
guide covers what to focus on when you first install the game.

## Open your daily packs

You can open booster packs for free every day. Opening packs is the primary
way to grow your collection, so make it a habit — pick one expansion and open
its packs consistently rather than spreading pulls across every expansion at
once.

## Learn the match basics

Matches in Pocket are faster than the tabletop Pokémon TCG:

| Rule | Pocket |
| ---- | ------ |
| Deck size | 20 cards |
| Prize condition | 3 points |
| Energy | Generated automatically each turn |
| Bench size | 3 Pokémon |

Complete the solo missions first; they teach the rules and pay out hourglasses
and pack currency you can spend on your collection.

## Build your first deck

Start from a deck the game suggests, then swap cards as your collection grows.
A typical starting shape is two evolution lines plus trainer cards that draw
or search — consistency beats raw power in a 20-card deck.
