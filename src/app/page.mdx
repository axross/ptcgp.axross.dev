# Pokémon TCG Pocket, documented

This site collects guides and reference material for **Pokémon TCG Pocket**
(PTCGP) — how the game works, how to build decks, and how to get the most out
of your collection.

Everything here is authored in [MDX](https://mdxjs.com), so pages can mix
prose with interactive components where a table or a widget explains
something better than words.

## Start here

- [Getting started](/guides/getting-started) — what Pokémon TCG Pocket is and
  what you should do in your first week.
